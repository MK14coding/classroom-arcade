World Cup Addition

Add the squad numbers on the players' shirts. As your score grows, the challenge gets harder.

Stage 1

Add 2 players. Reach a score of 20 to move to Stage 2.

Stage 2

Add 3 players. Reach a score of 30 to unlock Stage 3.

Stage 3

Add 4 players. Reach a score of 40 to win the World Cup.

How to Play

  • Look carefully at each player's squad number.
  • Add the numbers together and type your answer.
  • Press Check to see if you are correct.
  • You have 3 chances. Each wrong answer crosses out one goal.
  • If all 3 chances are used, your score resets and you go back to Stage 1.
  • Complete all stages to become the World Cup Champion.
